# How to Care for Your Money Tree

The Money Tree plant thrives when given proper care. Follow these tips to keep your plant healthy:

## Light Requirements
Money Trees prefer bright, indirect sunlight. Avoid direct sunlight as it can scorch the leaves.

## Watering
- Water your Money Tree once the top inch of soil has dried out.
- Ensure the pot has good drainage to prevent root rot.

## Humidity
Maintain moderate humidity levels, as Money Trees enjoy a tropical environment. Consider misting the plant occasionally.

## Fertilizing
Feed your Money Tree plant monthly during the growing season (spring and summer) with a balanced fertilizer.

## Pruning
- Trim brown or damaged leaves.
- Prune stems to shape the plant and promote growth.

With consistent care, your Money Tree plant will grow strong and healthy!
