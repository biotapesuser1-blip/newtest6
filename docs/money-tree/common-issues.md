# Troubleshooting Common Money Tree Problems

Despite being resilient, Money Tree plants can face challenges. Here’s how to resolve common issues:

## Yellowing Leaves
Yellow leaves can be a sign of overwatering. Check drainage and let the soil dry more between waterings.

## Brown Tips on Leaves
Brown tips often indicate low humidity levels. Mist the plant or use a humidifier.

## Droopy Leaves
Droopiness can result from inadequate watering or sudden environmental changes. Assess watering routine and placement.

## Pests
Money Trees are susceptible to spider mites or mealybugs. Treat infestations with insecticidal soap or by wiping leaves with a damp cloth.

## Rotting Braided Trunk
Overwatering can lead to trunk rot. Avoid water pooling around the base and ensure proper drainage.

These troubleshooting steps will support your Money Tree's recovery and long-term health.
